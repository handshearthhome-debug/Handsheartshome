Our Story

Where care
becomes craft.

"Soapmaking teaches what care looks like — measuring carefully, waiting patiently, creating something real to give someone else."

— Monica Walter, Founder
Shop Our Soaps

Hands Hearts & Home grew from something deeply personal — welcoming high-needs youth into our home and daily life. Soapmaking became a classroom.

Measuring oils teaches precision. Waiting for trace teaches patience. Unmolding a bar teaches pride. Each step builds confidence that lasts long after the soap is gone.

Every bar you bring home was made with the hands of young people who needed a purpose — and found one. Mission-driven, honest ingredients, made the right way.

What We Stand For

Real Ingredients

Tallow, premium clays, botanicals. Every ingredient earns its place — chosen because it genuinely works.

Youth-Made

Every product is touched by the young people in our care. Soapmaking gives them skills and pride that carries forward.

Purpose-Driven

Two collections, one mission. Meaningful work changes lives — and that begins right here in our home.
